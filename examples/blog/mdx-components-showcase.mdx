## Component Reference

### Button

The Button component supports three variants:

- `variant="primary"` - Blue background, white text
- `variant="secondary"` - Gray background, dark text
- `variant="text"` - Transparent background, blue text with hover underline

### Card

The Card component creates clickable cards:

- `title` - The card title
- `href` - The link destination
- `children` - The card content
