## Full-Screen Mode

For a full-screen presentation experience, use `<Slides>` without the `embedded` prop:

<Slides>
  <Slide>
    <h1>Full-Screen Presentation</h1>

    <p>Use arrow keys or click to navigate.</p>
  </Slide>

  <Slide>
    <h2>Second Slide</h2>
